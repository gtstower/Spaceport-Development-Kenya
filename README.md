Project Overview

This project focuses on the conceptual design, systems analysis, and trajectory optimization of space transportation infrastructure and launch mission operations using Systems Tool Kit (STK). The work integrates aerospace systems engineering, orbital mechanics, mission planning, and simulation-based optimization for modern spaceport operations and launch vehicle trajectory analysis.

The project combines:

Spaceport infrastructure development
Launch mission analysis
Orbital trajectory optimization
Space situational awareness concepts
Aerospace systems modeling
AI-assisted mission analysis
STK-based simulation and visualization

The research supports the development of efficient, safe, and scalable space transportation systems for future orbital missions.

Objectives

The major objectives of the project include:

Development of conceptual spaceport operational architectures
Analysis of launch site requirements and infrastructure systems
Modeling and optimization of launch vehicle trajectories
Simulation of orbital insertion missions using STK
Evaluation of launch constraints and mission feasibility
Integration of AI-assisted mission planning concepts
Assessment of trajectory performance metrics
Analysis of orbital safety and mission reliability
